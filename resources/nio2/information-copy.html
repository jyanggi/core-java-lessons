WELCOME TO YANGUAS MINDS

Hi, and welcome to Yanguas Minds.

This site is born from our heartfelt journey in teaching and homeschooling our two amazing children, Joaquin and Leone. While our eldest, Joaquin, has been diagnosed on the Autism Spectrum, we hold a deep belief that every child, no matter their challenges, has the potential to thrive and shine in their own unique way. Each mind is a world of its own—special, brilliant, and capable.

We acknowledge the irreplaceable role we play as their primary teachers, guiding them with love, patience, and determination. This site serves as both a shared platform where their Daddy John’s wisdom and insight is brought to life and a mirror of my own thoughts as their Mommy Jane. We also learn from each of our kids. They are our teachers in this life. Together, we all contribute to this learning journey—hence the name, Yanguas Minds. 

We hope you find our stories and resources meaningful and helpful as they are all made with love. Join us in our learning adventure!

Love lots,

Yanguas Family
